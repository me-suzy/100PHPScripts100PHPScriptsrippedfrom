Access Instructions for Your Affiliate Program:

For the Affiliates Section Login:
http://yourdomain.com/cgi-bin/login.cgi

To signup as a New Affiliate:
http://yourdomain.com/cgi-bin/signup.cgi

For the Administrative Section Login:
http://yourdomain.com/cgi-bin/admin/login.cgi

The New Thank You URL Link for your Products is:
http://yourdomain.com/cgi-bin/thankyou.cgi?Product_ID_Here

And you will be able to add/edit and/or remove Products thru the administrative section.
