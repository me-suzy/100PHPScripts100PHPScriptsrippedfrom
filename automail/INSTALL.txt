[Overview]

AutoMail lets you mail a series of unlimited emails 
that are sent out to prospective customers/consumers 
over a period of time designated in days by you. Setup 
is as simple as it gets. Emails are highly customizable, 
just create them in a text editor and upload to your 
server. AutoMail runs on your unix based webserver 
and allows you to send as many as you like every day of 
the year. 

#########################################################

[Features]

+EASY web administration interface!
+Create as many messages as you want.
+You set all of the mailing options to your liking.
+Keep your product or business FRESH in visitors' minds!
+Generate tons of repeat traffic to your site.
+Get more orders and signups from "reluctant" visitors!
+Users can sign themselves up for your mailings.
+Users can unsubscribe/remove themselves.
+Personalize messages with subscribers' names!

Installation instructions for AutoMail

Inside the zip you should find the following files :

  autoresponder.php - autoresponder (advanced users only) 
  common.php        - common functions and variables 
  config.php        - variables you must set 
  foot.php          - footer appended to output of pages 
  index.php         - AutoMail admin interface 
  install.php       - installation script 
  INSTALL.txt       - this file
  MANUAL.html       - installation/usage manual
  messages.php      - message editor 
  options.php       - preference editor 
  process.php       - sends and tracks daily messages 
  sample-subscribe-form.php - example self-subscribe form 
  user.php          - handles user functions  

Unzip the file and follow the directions in MANUAL.html
to install, configure, and use AutoMail.

#########################################################

[Usage]

Once AutoMail is installed, visit the index.php file
in its installed directory. Create your password, then
log in and begin using the script.
